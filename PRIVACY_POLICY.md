## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you use the App, you may create and store the following content locally on your device:

- **Bingo Card Content**: Goals, rewards, motivational text, design preferences, and game state (marked cells, claimed rewards)
- **App Settings**: Design selections, font preferences, and other customization choices

**Important**: All user-created content is stored **locally on your device only** using your device's local storage (SharedPreferences). We do **not** collect, transmit, or store your personal goals, rewards, or any other content you create in the App on our servers or with third parties.

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

#### Device Information
- **Device Identifiers**: Device type, model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Advertising Identifiers**: Google Advertising ID (GAID) on Android or Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) on iOS, which may be used for advertising purposes
- **Device Configuration**: Screen resolution, device language, time zone, and other device settings

#### Usage Information
- **App Usage Data**: Features accessed, interactions with the App, session duration, and performance metrics
- **Error and Diagnostics**: Crash reports, error logs, and diagnostic information that help us improve the App's stability

#### Network Information
- **IP Address**: Automatically collected when you use the App to connect to our servers or third-party services (Firebase, AdMob)
- **Network Type**: Information about your network connection (Wi-Fi, cellular)

### 2.3 Information Collected by Third-Party Services

#### Google AdMob
The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect the following information:

- Device identifiers (GAID/IDFA)
- IP address
- Device information (model, OS version)
- App usage data related to ad interactions
- Location data (approximate, based on IP address)

For more information about AdMob's data practices, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

#### Google Firebase/Firestore
The App uses Firebase Firestore to fetch design templates (SVG files) for bingo card customization. Firebase may collect:

- Device identifiers
- IP address
- Usage analytics (only for design template downloads)

**Important**: We do **not** upload your bingo card content, goals, rewards, or any personal information to Firebase. Firebase is used **only** to download publicly available design templates.

#### Google Fonts
The App uses Google Fonts to load custom fonts. When fonts are downloaded, Google may collect:

- IP address
- Device information
- Usage statistics

For more information, visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 4. AdMob and Personalized Advertising

### 4.1 Types of Ads

The App displays advertisements through Google AdMob, which may serve:

- **Personalized Ads**: Ads tailored to your interests based on your device identifiers, app usage, and other data collected by AdMob and its advertising partners
- **Non-Personalized Ads**: Ads that are not based on your interests or personal data but may still use contextual information, IP address, or coarse location

### 4.2 Your Advertising Choices

You have control over personalized advertising:

#### On Your Device
- **Android**: You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
- **iOS**: You can limit ad tracking in your device settings: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking

#### In the App
- We comply with applicable privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.) and may present you with consent choices when required by law
- If you are in a region that requires explicit consent (e.g., European Economic Area, UK), you will see a consent dialog before personalized ads are shown

### 4.3 GDPR Compliance for EU/UK Users

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, and Switzerland:

- We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and ePrivacy Directive
- We use Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) or a certified Consent Management Platform (CMP) to obtain your consent for personalized advertising where required
- You have the right to withdraw consent at any time
- If you do not consent to personalized ads, you will see non-personalized or limited ads instead

For more information about AdMob's GDPR compliance, visit: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/9760862

## 12. Deep Linking and Sharing Features

The App includes features that allow you to share your bingo card with others via deep links (e.g., `nybingo://import?data=...`). When you share a bingo card:

- The bingo card data is **encoded in the URL** and sent directly to the recipient
- The data is **not** stored on our servers or transmitted through our servers
- Sharing occurs **directly between devices** using standard device sharing mechanisms (e.g., clipboard, messaging apps, email)

**Important**: Be cautious when sharing bingo cards, as the recipient will be able to view all content in your bingo card. We are not responsible for how recipients use or store shared bingo card data.
